# Founding Intern Captain Playbook

## Who To Recruit

- Incoming or current NYC interns.
- Finance, consulting, business, tech, media, and startup interns.
- Club officers with access to dense student groups.
- Students already in company, school, or housing group chats.

## The Ask

You are helping form the 2026 NYC intern map before summer starts.

Targets per captain:
- 25 verified intern signups.
- 2 group chat drops.
- 1 LinkedIn, Instagram, or TikTok post.
- 1 intro to another potential captain.

## Reward

- Founding Intern Captain status.
- Early access when company pods and school crews unlock.
- Feature in the NYC Intern Pulse.
- Priority invite to first-week plans.

## Invite DM

Hey [Name] - quick idea. I am helping launch Internconnected, a verified NYC intern map for summer 2026 interns. It lets people add their school, company, and role so company pods and school crews can unlock before everyone arrives.

You seem plugged into [school/company/community], so I thought you could be a great Founding Intern Captain. The ask is simple: help get 25 real NYC interns to add themselves, mostly by sharing in chats you already use.

Want me to send the early link?

## Captain Onboarding Message

You are in. Your job is to help your school/company show up in the first NYC Intern Pulse.

Use this:

I joined Internconnected, the 2026 NYC intern map. If you are interning in NYC this summer, add your name, school, role, and company so our company/school pod can unlock:
[LINK]

Goal this week: 25 verified interns.

Do not overthink it. The best channels are the group chats where interns already are.
