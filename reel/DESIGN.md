## Style Prompt

Internconnected should feel like a crisp NYC field guide for ambitious summer interns: editorial map texture, confident startup energy, playful competition, and clear social proof. The reel uses the existing cream paper, deep ink, mint, red, blue, yellow, and plum palette from the website and attached assets.

## Colors

- Paper: #f8f4eb
- Ink: #071422
- Muted ink: #334155
- Mint: #58c68f
- Signal red: #ee5a43
- Signal blue: #2d8bd7
- Signal yellow: #f3bd37
- Plum: #9b72bb

## Typography

- Brand and UI: Avenir Next, Trebuchet MS, Arial, sans-serif
- Display fallback: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic neon tech gradients.
- Do not bury the actual product imagery behind abstract decoration.
- Do not use dark, moody stock-video styling.
- Do not make the CTA vague; the goal is signups.
